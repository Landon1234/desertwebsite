Desert

Photo via on Visualhunt.com

I picked the desert to tie in the cell parts to plants or living things because I am familiar with what lives and grows in the desert. I also found it interesting that even though there aren't very many plants in the desert that grow, the plants that do grow use methods similar to the methods that a cell uses.

Parts of the cell analogies
Nucleus The Nucleus is like the sun in the desert, it controls everything in the enviroment.
Mitocondria The Mitocondria is like a cactus, it stores the water much like the Mitocondria stores energy.
Vacule The Vacule is like the sand in the desert, it stores water the same as sand would store the water and heat.
Chloraplast Chloraplast is like a house running on solar pannles in the desert, both make their own energy.
Cell Membrane The Cell Membrane is like the security at the baorder, it regulates what would enter or leave the cell.
Cell Wall The cell Wall like the wall at the boarder, it fortifies the cell much like the wall would fortify the boarder.
Cytoplasm Cytoplasm is like the the ecosystem in the desert, the ecosystem shapes the desert like the cytoplasm shapes the cell.
Lysome Lysomes are like the prediters in the desert, they both work to get rid of the dead waste or animals taking up room in the cell or ecosystem.
Rybosome Rybosomes are like the water in the desert ecosystem, both are needed and depended on for life of the animals and the cell.
Golgi Golgi is like erosion, when erosion occurs it breaks down and sorts the particles and changes the terrain.
